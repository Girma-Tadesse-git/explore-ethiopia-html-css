Ethiopia Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Ethiopia is during the dry season from October to May. The weather is pleasant with clear skies, making it ideal for exploring historical sites and trekking in the mountains.

Visa Information

Most visitors to Ethiopia require a visa. You can apply for an e-visa online before your trip or get a visa on arrival at Addis Ababa Bole International Airport.

Currency

The Ethiopian Birr (ETB) is the local currency. It's advisable to carry some cash as credit cards are not widely accepted outside major hotels and establishments in Addis Ababa.

Health Precautions

Consult your doctor about vaccinations before traveling to Ethiopia. Yellow fever vaccination is required if you're arriving from a country with risk of yellow fever transmission.
